Slow Cooker Beef Stew

Description:

Easy to make slow cooker beef stew with vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat
  • 1/4 cup All-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups beef broth
  • 4 medium carrots, sliced
  • 3 medium potatoes, diced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 large bay leaf

Steps:

  1. Combine the ingredients; put the beef in the slow cooker, then toss with flour, salt, and pepper. Add broth, vegetables, Worcestershire sauce, paprika, garlic, and bay leaf. Stir to combine
  2. Set slow cooker, cover is and cook on low for 8 to 12 hours. or on high for 4 to 6 hours.
